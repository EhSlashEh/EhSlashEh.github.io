With a strong background in supply chain management and expertise in creating and maintaining standardized workflows, data management, reporting, and inventory system design, I've decided to explore my love for coding and expand my technical skills.

On this portfolio website, you'll find a collection of my projects, ranging from games built in Unity using C# to web development projects utilizing HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I'm excited to showcase how I've applied my engineering mindset to develop creative solutions and solve complex problems through programming.
